Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Doggo Pipeline is a personal content publishing tool used solely by its owner to post original creative video content to their own TikTok account. It is not a public service and is not available to other users.

Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from any individual. No user accounts, profiles, or personally identifiable information are created or retained.

TikTok API Usage

This app uses the TikTok Content Posting API exclusively to post videos to the account owner's own TikTok profile. No data is read from TikTok or any other user's account. Access tokens are stored locally on the owner's private machine and are never transmitted to any third party.

Third Parties

This application does not share any data with third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.
